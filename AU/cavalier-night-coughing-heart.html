Night coughing for 3 weeks - vet says possible heart disease

👤 Linda P. 📅 2026-07-11 🐕 Max, Cavalier King Charles Spaniel 💬 8 comments ❤️ 217 likes 👁 3200 views
10-year-old Cavalier King Charles Spaniel coughing at night. X-ray shows enlarged heart. Vet wants more tests. Seeking CKCS heart disease experiences.
Max has been coughing at night for about 3 weeks now. Started off as occasional, now it's almost every night. He sounds like he's trying to clear his throat. Vet did x-rays and mentioned something about an enlarged heart. She wants to do more tests next week but didn't seem overly alarmed. I'm absolutely terrified though. Has anyone had a CKCS with heart disease? What happened? How are they going now? I feel like I should be doing something right now but the vet said just to monitor. Is there anything I can do to help him sleep better at night?

Cavaliers and heart disease is such a known combo. Please push for a cardiac ultrasound not just a stethoscope check. Our Charlie's murmur was missed twice before someone finally did an echo.

The coughing after exercise is a classic sign of an enlarged heart putting pressure on the airway. Please get the full cardiac workup — echo, ECG, chest x-ray if you can.
