Veterinarian's Insight: NT-proBNP Fills a Gap in Heartworm Staging

heart 2026-07-21

Dr. José A. Montoya-Alonso, lead researcher of the 2023 study, emphasizes that while echocardiography is the standard for diagnosing pulmonary hypertension (PH) in heartworm disease, it requires specialized equipment and expertise not always available in general practice. NT-proBNP offers a complementary, accessible blood test that can flag dogs likely to have moderate-severe PH. 'This biomarker helps us identify animals in more advanced stages, even when clinical signs are subtle,' he notes. Integrating NT-proBNP into routine heartworm workups allows earlier intervention and better risk stratification before initiating adulticide therapy.
