Warning: Avoid Over-Supplementing Joint Products

joint 2026-07-16

It is a common mistake to give multiple joint supplements simultaneously without checking ingredients. Over-supplementing can lead to toxicity or side effects. For example, too much fish oil (exceeding 100 mg EPA/DHA per kg daily) may cause vomiting, diarrhea, or delayed blood clotting. High doses of turmeric can irritate the stomach or interact with blood thinners. Some supplements also contain hidden NSAIDs or steroids. Always read labels carefully, consult your vet, and stick to one clinically tested product. More is not better—proper dosing ensures safety and effectiveness for your dog's joints.
