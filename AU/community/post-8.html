Thanks for sharing. For dogs with heart disease, consistency with prescribed medication is the single biggest lever - same time daily, never skip. Low-sodium diet, a calm environment, and avoiding hot-weather exertion all reduce cardiac load. Watch for increased coughing, breathing changes, or fainting and report them promptly.
