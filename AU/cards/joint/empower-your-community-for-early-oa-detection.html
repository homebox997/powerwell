Empower Your Community for Early OA Detection

joint 2026-07-16

Veterinary teams and pet owners can work together to improve early detection of osteoarthritis. By planting seeds of awareness in the community—through client education, questionnaires, and screening exams—more dogs can be diagnosed before mobility is severely impaired. Early detection is the first line of defense in proactive OA management, leading to better long-term outcomes and a higher quality of life for affected dogs.
