Myth Busted: Joint Pain Is Not Just Old Age

joint 2026-07-16

A common misconception is that joint pain and stiffness in dogs are just normal signs of aging and cannot be treated. In reality, osteoarthritis is a medical condition that causes chronic pain and inflammation. While age is a risk factor, proper management—including medication, supplements, and lifestyle changes—can significantly improve a dog's comfort and mobility. Ignoring symptoms can lead to worsening pain and reduced quality of life.
