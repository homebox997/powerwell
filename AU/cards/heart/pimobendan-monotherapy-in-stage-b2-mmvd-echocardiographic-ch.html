Pimobendan Monotherapy in Stage B2 MMVD: Echocardiographic Changes

heart 2026-07-18

A study on dogs with Stage B2 myxomatous mitral valve disease (MMVD) treated with pimobendan monotherapy showed significant echocardiographic improvements. Key findings included reductions in left atrial and ventricular dimensions, indicating decreased cardiac remodeling. The treatment also improved fractional shortening and ejection fraction, suggesting enhanced systolic function. These results support pimobendan as an effective sole therapy for managing early-stage MMVD in dogs, potentially delaying progression to congestive heart failure.
