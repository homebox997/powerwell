Early Signs of Canine Osteoarthritis

joint 2026-07-16

Canine osteoarthritis (OA) is a common but often underdiagnosed cause of chronic pain. Early indicators include limping, stiffness, and subtle behavior changes such as reluctance to jump, climb stairs, or play. Dogs may also show decreased activity, irritability, or changes in sleep patterns. Recognizing these signs early is critical for proactive management and improving long-term outcomes.
