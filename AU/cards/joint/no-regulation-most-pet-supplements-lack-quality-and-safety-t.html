No Regulation: Most Pet Supplements Lack Quality and Safety Testing

joint 2026-07-16

Unlike pharmaceutical drugs, nutraceuticals (joint supplements) are not subject to strict FDA oversight for efficacy, safety, or manufacturing quality. The only regulation they must follow is that they cannot legally claim to treat any specific condition. Most products do not undergo rigorous testing to confirm that the ingredients listed on the label are present in the stated amounts—or whether they are present at all. This means pet owners cannot assume a product is pure or effective. To ensure safety, choose brands that provide third-party testing and are recommended by your veterinarian.
