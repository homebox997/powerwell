Dr. Leilani Alvarez: Rely on Science, Not Hype, for Joint Health

joint 2026-07-16

CARE contributor Dr. Leilani Alvarez advises pet parents to rely on science and not hype when choosing joint supplements. With a growing number of options on the market, it is essential to look for products that are supported by clinical research and recommended by your veterinarian. She emphasizes that just because a product is labeled 'natural' does not mean it is safe. Pets metabolize supplements through their liver and kidneys, so safety testing is crucial. Always ask for products with proven purity and efficacy, and consult your vet before starting any new supplement regimen.
