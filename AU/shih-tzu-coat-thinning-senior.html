Isapooy coat has gone really thin - should I panic?

👤 Rachel K. 📅 2026-07-11 🐕 Coco, Shih Tzu 💬 8 comments ❤️ 219 likes 👁 2400 views
10-year-old Shih Tzu coat has thinned dramatically. Otherwise healthy. Wondering if thyroid or just normal ageing. Seeking advice.
Coco's beautiful fluffy coat has really thinned out over the last 6 months. You can almost see her skin through it now. She's always had the most gorgeous double coat and now she just looks... old. She's 10, otherwise healthy, eating fine, same energy. But the coat thing really bothers me. Is this just normal ageing or could it be a thyroid issue? Vet's not concerned but I am. Has anyone had their senior Shih Tzu go through this? What helped?

Nina B. 2026-07-11

Shih tzus and coat thinning is so common in seniors. Could be thyroid (hypothyroidism) — worth a full thyroid panel not just the basic blood test. Our poodle's coat thickened up heaps once on thyroid meds.

Dave T. 2026-07-11

Cushing's disease also causes coat changes and increased thirst. Both are testable. Ask your vet for a CBC + chemistry + thyroid panel together — it's about $180 all up.

Karen H. 2026-07-11

Our lab's coat thinned at 11 but it turned out to be nutritional — she wasn't absorbing zinc well. Zinc supplementation fixed it within 3 months!

Tracy L. 2026-07-11

Gentle grooming with a wide-tooth comb only — no slicker brushes on thinning coats. Pat dry don't rub. Our golden's coat actually recovered some density when we stopped being so rough with brushing.

Brett C. 2026-07-11

Omega 3 supplements (fish oil) can really help coat quality. We use salmon oil from the pet shop, about $25 for a big bottle that lasts 2 months.

Rob F. 2026-07-11

Has anyone tried the braided salmon oil + vitamin E combo? Our border collie's coat went from dull and thin to actually shiny in about 6 weeks.

Gary M. 2026-07-11

Hair loss around the flanks and back legs specifically is a classic hypothyroid pattern in dogs. This sounds like it might be worth that vet visit.

Jen W. 2026-07-11

Our cavoodle lost coat on her sides at 10. Turns out she had adrenal issues. $200 ultrasound and a $35/month med later and the coat came back!
