Multimodal Therapy: Best Approach for Canine Arthritis

heart 2026-07-18

Managing dog joint disease requires a combination of therapies rather than a single treatment. Core components include NSAIDs (non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs) prescribed by a vet for pain and inflammation, alongside joint supplements like glucosamine and omega-3 fatty acids. Physical therapy such as hydrotherapy and therapeutic laser can improve mobility. Weight management is essential. In advanced cases, surgical options like joint replacement or arthrodesis may be considered. Always work with a veterinarian to tailor a plan to your dog's specific condition and stage of disease.
