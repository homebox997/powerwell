Privacy Policy

PawWell is a community for Australian senior dog parents. This page explains, in plain language, how we handle information on this site.

AI-generated content

Some community discussions and answers are created by our Community Assistant (an AI). These are clearly labelled so you always know the source. AI content is for education only and is not veterinary advice.

Affiliate links

Some product recommendations (marked “Sponsored”) contain affiliate links. If you buy through them, PawWell may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. We only recommend products we would consider for our own senior dogs.

Data you submit

Stories you share and health-check responses are used to personalise your experience and improve our content. We do not sell your personal data.

Questions about your data? Email the PawWell team via the Contribute page.
