Omega-3 Fatty Acids: The Most Effective Joint Supplement

joint 2026-07-16

A 2022 systematic review of 72 clinical studies found that omega-3 fatty acid supplements (such as fish oil and green-lipped mussel extract) were the most effective nutraceuticals for managing osteoarthritis in pets. Nearly all trials showed significant improvement or full pain relief compared to placebo. Omega-3s work by reducing inflammation in the joints. However, it is critical to choose a pure product free from toxins and heavy metals. Always consult your veterinarian for a recommended brand and proper dosage for your pet's specific needs.
