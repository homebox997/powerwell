Know Your Breed's Normal: A First Step in Prevention

joint 2026-07-16

Effective posture-based injury prevention starts with knowing what 'normal' looks like for your specific dog breed. A greyhound's natural standing posture is very different from a terrier's. To establish this baseline, consult your country's Kennel Club or breed standard resources. Once you know your dog's ideal structure, you can more easily spot early deviations that signal potential problems. This knowledge empowers you to take early action with targeted exercises, potentially preventing minor postural issues from developing into serious orthopaedic conditions like arthritis or ligament tears.
