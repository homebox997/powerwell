Veterinarian Consensus: Early MMVD Treatment Saves Lives

heart 2026-07-20

The ACVIM consensus panel, composed of leading veterinary cardiologists, strongly recommends early treatment for dogs with myxomatous mitral valve disease (MMVD) once congestive heart failure develops. They emphasize that pimobendan should be started as soon as heart failure is diagnosed, as it has been shown to significantly prolong survival and improve quality of life. The panel also advises against using certain medications like ACE inhibitors before heart failure onset, unless specifically indicated. Their expert view is that tailored, stage-based treatment plans are essential for managing this progressive disease effectively.
