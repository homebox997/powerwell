Omega-3s in Cats: Encouraging Results but More Research Needed

joint 2026-07-16

While most clinical trials on joint supplements focus on dogs, the few studies available on cats—specifically those involving omega-3 fatty acids—showed encouraging results for managing feline osteoarthritis. This is a positive sign for cat owners, suggesting that omega-3 supplementation may help reduce joint pain and improve mobility in cats as well. However, researchers emphasize that more high-quality studies are needed to confirm these benefits and to establish optimal dosing guidelines for feline patients. Always consult your veterinarian before giving any supplement to your cat.
