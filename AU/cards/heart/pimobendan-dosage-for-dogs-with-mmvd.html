Pimobendan Dosage for Dogs with MMVD

heart 2026-07-20

Pimobendan is a key medication for dogs with myxomatous mitral valve disease (MMVD) and congestive heart failure. The recommended dosage is 0.25–0.3 mg per kilogram of body weight, given orally twice daily, approximately 12 hours apart. It should be administered on an empty stomach (at least one hour before or two hours after a meal) for optimal absorption. The dose should not be doubled if a dose is missed; simply give the next scheduled dose. Always consult a veterinarian before starting or adjusting this medication, as individual needs may vary based on disease severity.
