Max's Story: ESWT Helped His Stiff Knee But Not Fully

joint 2026-07-16

Max, a 9-year-old Labrador Retriever with severe stifle osteoarthritis, was enrolled in a shockwave therapy trial after failing to respond to standard pain medications. He received two sessions of focused ESWT 28 days apart. His owner noticed a slight improvement in his willingness to go on short walks, and objective gait analysis showed a small increase in vertical impulse (weight-bearing) on his affected leg. However, Max still showed morning stiffness and occasional lameness after exercise. While not a dramatic recovery, the therapy provided modest relief without side effects, giving him a bit more comfort in his senior years.
