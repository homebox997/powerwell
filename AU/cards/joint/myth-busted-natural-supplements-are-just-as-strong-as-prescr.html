Myth Busted: Natural Supplements Are Just as Strong as Prescription Painkillers

joint 2026-07-16

A common misconception is that natural joint supplements are too weak to replace prescription NSAIDs for dog arthritis. However, this study shows that EAB-277, a natural extract from green-lipped mussel and krill oil, performed equally well in reducing pain and improving function compared to NSAIDs. While not every supplement is equal, scientifically tested formulations can be highly effective. Owners should not dismiss natural options as placebos; instead, they should look for evidence-based products that have undergone rigorous clinical trials.

Tags: myth vs fact, natural supplement efficacy, NSAIDs vs supplements, canine arthritis myth, evidence-based pet care
Source: Frontiers | Evaluation of the comparative efficacy of green lipped mussel plus krill oil extracts (EAB-277), Biota orientalis extracts or NSAIDs for the treatment of dogs with osteoarthritis associated pain: a blinded, placebo-controlled study
