Common question: what is the life expectancy after a heart murmur diagnosis? It varies widely by stage. Many dogs live happily for years with early-stage murmurs and good management; later stages need closer care. Staging (listening, X-ray, echo) guides the plan - ask your vet which stage your dog is at.
