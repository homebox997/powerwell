Postural Exercises for Canine Rehabilitation

joint 2026-07-16

Once a veterinarian or canine physiotherapist has assessed your dog, specific postural exercises can be prescribed. A foundational exercise is teaching a 'tidy sit' by positioning your dog against a wall with their weakest hind leg closest, encouraging them to keep the leg close to the body. Another key exercise is weight shifting: with your dog in a perfect standing posture, gently and slowly sway their body from side to side using your hands over their thighs or shoulder blades. This strengthens the smallest postural muscles. These exercises should be performed consistently for 5-10 minutes daily.
