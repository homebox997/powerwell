Myth: Good Posture Only Matters for Show Dogs

joint 2026-07-16

Many owners believe that posture is only a concern for conformation show dogs. This is a myth. While breed standards define 'normal' structure, functional posture affects every dog's joint health and injury resilience. A dog with poor standing posture—like a roached back or uneven weight distribution—is at higher risk for strains, arthritis, and other orthopaedic issues regardless of their breed or show career. Learning to identify and improve your pet's posture is a powerful, accessible tool for any dog owner to enhance their companion's long-term well-being and quality of life.
