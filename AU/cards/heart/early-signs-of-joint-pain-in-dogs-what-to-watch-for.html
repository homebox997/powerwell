Early Signs of Joint Pain in Dogs: What to Watch For

heart 2026-07-18

Dogs with joint issues often show subtle changes first. Look for stiffness after rest, especially in the morning or after naps. You may notice a reduced willingness to jump on furniture or climb stairs. Lameness that shifts from one leg to another, a bunny-hopping gait when running, or lagging behind on walks are common signs. Dogs may also lick or chew at specific joints and show behavioral changes like increased irritability or reluctance to be touched. These symptoms often worsen in cold or damp weather.
