Advanced Tools: Balance Pads and Incline Training

joint 2026-07-16

To further enhance postural rehabilitation, canine rehabilitation specialists often recommend advanced tools. Foam balance pads and wobble cushions create unstable surfaces that challenge a dog's proprioception and core stability, strengthening deep postural muscles. Performing exercises on gentle inclines or declines also provides a unique stimulus, targeting different muscle groups. These tools should only be introduced after a professional assessment and when the dog has mastered basic floor exercises. A specialist can provide personalized guidance, making workouts safer and more effective for your dog's specific needs.
