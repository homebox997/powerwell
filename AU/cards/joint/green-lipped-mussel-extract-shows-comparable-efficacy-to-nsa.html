Green-Lipped Mussel Extract Shows Comparable Efficacy to NSAIDs in Dog OA

joint 2026-07-16

A blinded, placebo-controlled study evaluated the comparative efficacy of EAB-277 (a combination of green-lipped mussel and krill oil extracts) against NSAIDs (non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs) for treating osteoarthritis-associated pain in dogs. The results indicated that EAB-277 was as effective as NSAIDs in reducing pain and improving mobility, offering a natural alternative with fewer side effects. This clinical data supports the use of marine-based supplements as a viable option for managing chronic joint pain in canines.
