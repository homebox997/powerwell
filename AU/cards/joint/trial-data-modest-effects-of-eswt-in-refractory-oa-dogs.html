Trial Data: Modest Effects of ESWT in Refractory OA Dogs

joint 2026-07-16

A randomized double-blinded trial involving 24 client-owned dogs with refractory elbow or stifle osteoarthritis evaluated one versus two sessions of focused ESWT. Objective measures included kinetic gait analysis (peak vertical pressure and vertical impulse), joint range of motion, and limb circumference. Results showed small improvements: vertical impulse and limb circumference increased more consistently in dogs receiving two sessions, while peak vertical pressure increased in both groups, including before treatment in the single-session group. No sustained improvement was seen in symmetry or range of motion. Owner-reported pain scores varied without consistent treatment effects.
