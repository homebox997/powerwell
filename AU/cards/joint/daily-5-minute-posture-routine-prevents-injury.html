Daily 5-Minute Posture Routine Prevents Injury

joint 2026-07-16

You don't need special equipment to proactively protect your dog's joints. Just 5-10 minutes of consistent, targeted postural exercises each day can significantly reduce the risk of future orthopaedic injuries. By teaching your dog to stand, sit, and lie down with correct alignment, you strengthen the core and postural muscles that support the spine and joints. This proactive approach builds a strong 'foundation' for your dog's body, making them more resistant to breed-specific conditions. Early detection of poor posture also allows for timely intervention before minor issues become major problems.
