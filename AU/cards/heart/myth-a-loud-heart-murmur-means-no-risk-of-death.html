Myth: A Loud Heart Murmur Means No Risk of Death

heart 2026-07-18

It is a common misconception that a louder heart murmur always indicates a worse prognosis. In dogs with myxomatous mitral valve disease (MMVD), a louder murmur at presentation was not found to be associated with increased risk of in-hospital death. However, it was linked to a higher likelihood of re-hospitalization for cardiogenic pulmonary edema within two months. Therefore, while a loud murmur may not predict immediate fatality, it does signal a need for vigilant follow-up to prevent recurrent heart failure episodes.
