Max the Labrador: How Weight Loss Transformed His Joints

heart 2026-07-18

Max, a 7-year-old Labrador Retriever, struggled with hip arthritis and could barely climb stairs. He weighed 95 pounds, 20 pounds over his ideal weight. His owner started a strict diet plan with measured meals and replaced treats with carrots and green beans. Combined with daily swimming sessions, Max lost 18 pounds over 6 months. Within 8 weeks, his lameness improved, and he began jumping onto the couch again. His vet reduced his anti-inflammatory medication by half. This case shows that weight loss alone can dramatically improve joint function in overweight dogs.
