Warning: 'Natural' Supplements Can Be Unsafe for Pets

joint 2026-07-16

Just because a joint supplement is labeled 'natural' does not mean it is safe for your pet. Pets metabolize supplements through their liver and kidneys, just like medications. This raises critical safety concerns: Are these products safe for pets with liver or kidney disease? Are they safe to use alongside other medications? Most supplement manufacturers are not required to answer these questions through research or testing. Furthermore, there is little oversight to ensure products are free from contaminants like heavy metals, pesticides, or other toxins. Always consult your vet before starting any supplement.
