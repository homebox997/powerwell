Veterinarian: Use Multimodal Lab Testing for Heart Disease

heart 2026-07-19

Dr. Gavazza and colleagues emphasize that no single lab test is perfect for canine heart disease. Traditional tests like CK, AST, and LDH are non-specific and have short half-lives. Specific biomarkers (troponin, natriuretic peptides) offer better sensitivity but must be interpreted with clinical signs and imaging. A multimodal approach combining blood work, echocardiography, and X-rays gives the fullest picture. This strategy helps detect early disease, monitor progression, and adjust therapy effectively.
