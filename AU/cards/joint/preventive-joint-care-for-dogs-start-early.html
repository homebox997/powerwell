Preventive Joint Care for Dogs: Start Early

joint 2026-07-16

Preventing canine osteoarthritis begins with maintaining a healthy weight, as excess body fat increases joint stress and inflammation. Regular, low-impact exercise like swimming or leash walks helps strengthen muscles around joints without causing damage. Provide a balanced diet rich in omega-3 fatty acids and antioxidants to support joint health. For high-risk breeds (e.g., large or giant dogs), consider joint-supporting supplements from puppyhood. Avoid repetitive high-impact activities (e.g., jumping on hard surfaces) and use non-slip flooring at home to reduce joint strain. Early preventive measures can delay OA onset and improve lifelong mobility.
