Osteoarthritis: One of the Most Common Chronic Conditions in Pets

joint 2026-07-16

Osteoarthritis (OA) is a highly prevalent chronic condition affecting both dogs and cats. It causes progressive joint degeneration, leading to pain, stiffness, and reduced mobility. Pet owners may notice their pet has difficulty getting up, is reluctant to jump or climb stairs, or shows a decreased interest in play. Early recognition of these signs is crucial for managing the disease and improving quality of life. If your pet shows these symptoms, a veterinary diagnosis is essential before starting any treatment plan.
