Early Supplementation with Omega-3s May Prevent Canine Joint Deterioration

joint 2026-07-16

Preventative joint care in dogs can include early supplementation with omega-3 fatty acids from sources like green-lipped mussel and krill oil. The study highlights that such natural extracts have anti-inflammatory properties that may slow the progression of osteoarthritis. Starting supplements before significant joint damage occurs can help maintain cartilage health and reduce future pain. Owners of at-risk breeds or aging dogs should consult their veterinarian about incorporating these supplements into their pet's diet as a proactive measure.
