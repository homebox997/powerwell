Joint Supplement Basics: Glucosamine and Omega-3 Dosage Tips

joint 2026-07-16

While not covered in the shockwave study, joint supplements are a cornerstone of canine OA care. Common supplements include glucosamine (typically 15-20 mg per pound of body weight daily) and chondroitin (10-15 mg per pound). Omega-3 fatty acids from fish oil (EPA/DHA) are also recommended at about 20-25 mg per pound combined daily, as they help reduce inflammation. Always choose products formulated for dogs and consult your vet for personalized dosing, as overweight dogs or those with certain health conditions may need adjustments. Supplements work best when combined with weight management and regular low-impact exercise.
