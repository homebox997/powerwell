Fibrosis Differences in DCM vs. CMVD Dog Hearts

heart 2026-07-21

A histopathologic study compared left atrial specimens from 16 dogs with dilated cardiomyopathy (DCM) and 15 dogs with chronic mitral valve disease (CMVD). Results showed significantly more interstitial fibrosis in the DCM group (median 4 vs. 2.5 grid fields; p < 0.05), but less perivascular fibrosis (median score 1 vs. 2; p < 0.05) compared to the CMVD group. These findings indicate that the pattern of connective tissue remodeling differs between the two diseases, helping veterinarians understand the underlying pathology and tailor treatment strategies.
