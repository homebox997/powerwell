No Supplement Can Replace Heartworm Prevention or Treatment

heart 2026-07-21

There are no scientifically validated supplements that prevent or treat heartworm disease or its complication, pulmonary hypertension. Some owners mistakenly try herbal remedies, high-dose antioxidants, or immune boosters hoping to avoid conventional treatment. This is dangerous. Heartworm adulticide therapy (melarsomine) is the only approved, effective treatment. For dogs with pulmonary hypertension, veterinarians may prescribe sildenafil (typically 1-3 mg/kg orally every 8-12 hours) or other targeted therapies—never as a substitute for adulticide. Always consult your veterinarian for evidence-based protocols; supplements alone will not eliminate adult heartworms or reverse vascular damage.
