Supplement Dosage: Fish Oil and ASU for Dogs

joint 2026-07-16

In the 2025 study, the oral supplement was given as one tablet per 10 kg body weight daily, providing approximately 300 mg fish oil (rich in EPA/DHA), 100 mg avocado/soybean unsaponifiables (ASU), 50 mg turmeric extract, 30 mg Boswellia extract, and 20 mg grape seed extract per 10 kg. For general joint support, vets often recommend 20-30 mg EPA/DHA per kg body weight daily from fish oil, and ASU at 10-15 mg per kg. Always start with the manufacturer's dosage and adjust under veterinary guidance. Do not exceed recommended amounts, as high doses of fish oil can cause gastrointestinal upset or bleeding risk.

Source: Frontiers | A randomized, double-blind, controlled study on the efficacy of an oral dietary supplement containing fish oil, ASU and phytotherapeutic extracts in canine osteoarthritis
