Vet Expert: Knee Pain in Dogs Often Signals Hip Problems

joint 2026-07-16

Veterinarians often note that young dogs and puppies have difficulty localizing leg pain. If a dog is limping but pointing to their knee, the actual problem may be in the hip. This is similar to the human pediatric rule that 'knee pain equals hip pain.' Conditions like slipped capital femoral epiphysis (SCFE) or hip dysplasia can manifest as knee-area lameness. A thorough orthopedic exam and X-rays of the hips are essential for an accurate diagnosis. Never assume a limp is 'just a knee issue' without professional evaluation.
