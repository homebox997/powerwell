When did you switch your dog to senior food? Is 7 actually old?

👤 Mark H. 📅 2026-07-11 🐕 Bella, Border Collie 💬 8 comments ❤️ 198 likes 👁 3490 views
7-year-old Border Collie still acts like a puppy. Vet mentioned senior food. Is 7 actually old for a collie? Struggling with the mental shift.
Bella's 7 and the vet mentioned she should be on senior food. I looked at the label and it said for dogs 7+. She doesn't look old to me at all - still runs around like a maniac at the park. Is 7 actually old for a border collie? I keep thinking about when I got her as a puppy and it feels like yesterday. When did you make the switch? Does it actually matter that much? Genuinely struggling with the mental shift of thinking of her as a senior dog.

Rob F. 2026-07-11

7 is the classic age to start thinking about senior food. Border collies age a bit faster than some breeds so 6.5-7 is the sweet spot for switching.

Karen H. 2026-07-11

Not all senior foods are created equal — heaps of them are just higher protein marketing with the same fillers. Read the ingredients! Look for real meat as first ingredient and lower phosphorus if kidney health is a concern.

Gary M. 2026-07-11

We switched our GSD at 8 to a senior formula with added joint support (glucosamine already in the food). Saved us giving a separate supplement.

Brett C. 2026-07-11

Slow transition over 7-10 days is critical. 25% new + 75% old for 3 days, then 50/50, etc. Sudden switches cause digestive upset.

Tracy L. 2026-07-11

Our golden transitioned easily but she was on a pretty bland diet anyway. Picky eaters can be trickier — maybe mix in some warm water to release the food's aroma.

Nina B. 2026-07-11

Our poodle turned his nose up at three different senior foods before accepting the Royal Canin Mature Adult. He's 11 now and doing brilliantly on it.

Dave T. 2026-07-11

Some dogs genuinely don't need senior food until 9 or 10 if they're healthy and active. Check with your vet — premature switching can mean unnecessary phosphorus restrictions.

Sue P. 2026-07-11

We switched our kelpie at 7 to a working dog senior formula (higher protein than most senior foods which can be too low for active breeds). Talk to your vet about whether standard senior food has enough protein for your BC.
