Complete guide: what I feed my 13-year-old staffy every single day

Roscoe's 13 and still going strong. I get a lot of questions about what he eats so here's my daily feeding breakdown.

Morning: 1 cup senior kibble (low calorie because he's less active) + half a tin of wet food mixed in Afternoon: Sardines in spring water (small tin) - great for omega 3s Evening: 1 cup senior kibble + a spoonful of boiled chicken Plus: Glucosamine supplement in his dinner, fresh water changed 3 times a day

He's maintained a healthy weight, coat's in great condition, vet says his bloods are excellent. I know every dog is different but happy to share more details if useful.
