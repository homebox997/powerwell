Veterinary Cardiologists Recommend Pimobendan for Stage B2 MMVD

heart 2026-07-18

Veterinary cardiologists increasingly recommend pimobendan monotherapy for dogs with Stage B2 myxomatous mitral valve disease based on evidence of improved echocardiographic outcomes. The study highlights that pimobendan effectively reduces left heart dimensions and enhances systolic function without the need for additional medications in early stages. Experts advise that early treatment can slow disease progression, making regular cardiac screening essential for susceptible breeds to initiate therapy at the optimal time.
