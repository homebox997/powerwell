Warning: Do Not Use Pimobendan in Dogs with Hypertrophic Cardiomyopathy

heart 2026-07-18

Pimobendan is contraindicated in dogs with hypertrophic cardiomyopathy (HCM) because it increases contractility and can worsen the condition. Unlike MMVD, which involves volume overload and chamber dilation, HCM features thickened heart muscle and impaired relaxation. Using pimobendan in HCM dogs may lead to reduced cardiac output or arrhythmias. Always confirm the specific type of heart disease through echocardiography before starting pimobendan therapy to avoid serious adverse effects.
