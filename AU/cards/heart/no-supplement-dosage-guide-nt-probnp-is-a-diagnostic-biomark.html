No Supplement Dosage Guide: NT-proBNP Is a Diagnostic Biomarker, Not a Supplement

heart 2026-07-21

NT-proBNP is a serum biomarker, not a supplement or medication. It is a protein fragment released by heart muscle cells when they are stretched, such as in pulmonary hypertension. In this study, a cutoff of ≥ 1178.45 pmol/l helped identify moderate-severe PH in heartworm-infected dogs. There is no dosage guide for NT-proBNP; rather, veterinarians measure its concentration in blood samples to assess heart health. Owners should not confuse biomarkers with treatments. Always consult a veterinarian for proper diagnosis and management of heartworm disease and associated complications.
