Never Give Human Painkillers to Dogs: Life-Threatening Danger

heart 2026-07-18

Giving dogs human pain relievers like ibuprofen, naproxen, or acetaminophen can be fatal. These medications are not safe for dogs and can cause severe gastrointestinal ulcers, kidney failure, and liver damage. Ibuprofen, for example, is highly toxic even in small doses. A single 200 mg tablet can poison a 20-pound dog. Signs of toxicity include vomiting, diarrhea, lethargy, and loss of appetite. If your dog shows signs of joint pain, always consult a veterinarian for approved canine NSAIDs. Never self-medicate your pet.
