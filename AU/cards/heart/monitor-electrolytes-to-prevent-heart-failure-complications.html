Monitor Electrolytes to Prevent Heart Failure Complications

heart 2026-07-19

Prevent severe electrolyte imbalances in dogs with heart disease by regular blood testing. Hypokalemia can cause arrhythmias, especially in dogs on diuretics. Hyponatremia may worsen with water retention and heart failure. Hyperkalemia can occur if kidneys fail. Work with your vet to adjust diet and medications. Avoiding sudden changes in fluid intake or diuretic doses helps maintain stable electrolytes.
