EAB-277: A Natural Supplement for Managing Canine Osteoarthritis Pain

joint 2026-07-16

EAB-277, a proprietary blend of green-lipped mussel and krill oil extracts, has been clinically shown to reduce osteoarthritis pain in dogs. In a controlled study, it performed comparably to traditional NSAIDs, making it a promising natural treatment option. This supplement works by providing omega-3 fatty acids and other anti-inflammatory compounds that help reduce joint inflammation and stiffness. Dogs receiving EAB-277 showed significant improvements in pain scores and mobility, suggesting it can be integrated into long-term management plans for canine arthritis.

Tags: EAB-277, green-lipped mussel, krill oil, osteoarthritis treatment, natural anti-inflammatory, dog arthritis
Source: Frontiers | Evaluation of the comparative efficacy of green lipped mussel plus krill oil extracts (EAB-277), Biota orientalis extracts or NSAIDs for the treatment of dogs with osteoarthritis associated pain: a blinded, placebo-controlled study
