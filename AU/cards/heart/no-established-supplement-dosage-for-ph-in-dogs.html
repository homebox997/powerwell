No Established Supplement Dosage for PH in Dogs

heart 2026-07-18

Currently, there are no established supplement dosage guidelines specifically for managing pulmonary hypertension (PH) in dogs with myxomatous mitral valve disease (MMVD). While some supplements like omega-3 fatty acids, taurine, and L-carnitine may support overall heart health, they are not proven to treat PH directly. The primary treatment for PH secondary to MMVD involves addressing the underlying heart disease, such as through mitral valve repair surgery or medical management with pimobendan, diuretics, and ACE inhibitors. Always consult a veterinary cardiologist before adding any supplements to your dog's heart disease treatment plan.
