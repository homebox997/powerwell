The CARE Quad Framework for Tailored Therapy

joint 2026-07-16

The CARE Quad framework is a tool to help veterinary professionals and pet owners select evidence-based therapies for canine osteoarthritis. It considers four key areas: pain management, physical therapy, weight management, and environmental modifications. Using this framework, care can be customized to the dog's specific OA stage and the owner's capabilities. In early OA, non-drug interventions like controlled exercise and home adjustments are often prioritized before medications.
