Recognizing Osteoarthritis Pain in Dogs: Key Signs to Watch For

joint 2026-07-16

Osteoarthritis in dogs often presents as stiffness, limping, reluctance to move, difficulty rising from a lying position, and decreased activity levels. Dogs may also show behavioral changes such as irritability or withdrawal. In the study, pain was assessed using validated owner questionnaires and veterinary exams. Early recognition of these signs is crucial for timely intervention. If your dog shows hesitation to jump, climb stairs, or play, they may be suffering from joint pain and should be evaluated for osteoarthritis.
