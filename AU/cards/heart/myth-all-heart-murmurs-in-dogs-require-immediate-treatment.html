Myth: All Heart Murmurs in Dogs Require Immediate Treatment

heart 2026-07-18

It is a common misconception that every dog with a heart murmur needs immediate medication. In reality, many murmurs are innocent or occur in early-stage disease where treatment is not yet indicated. The study focuses on Stage B2 MMVD, where dogs have significant heart enlargement but no symptoms. Treatment with pimobendan is only recommended at this specific stage. A veterinarian should evaluate the murmur's severity and stage before starting any therapy, avoiding unnecessary medication for mild cases.
