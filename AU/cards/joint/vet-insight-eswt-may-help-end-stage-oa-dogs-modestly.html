Vet Insight: ESWT May Help End-Stage OA Dogs Modestly

joint 2026-07-16

According to veterinary researchers involved in a 2026 double-blinded trial on canine osteoarthritis, extracorporeal shockwave therapy (ESWT) is safe and well tolerated, but its benefits in dogs with refractory elbow or stifle OA are limited. Dr. Susanne K. Lauer and her team noted that improvements were small and mainly seen in selected objective measures like vertical impulse, particularly after two treatment sessions. They emphasized that ESWT should not be expected to produce dramatic functional recovery in end-stage OA. Future studies with larger groups, optimized protocols, and longer follow-up are needed to clarify its role in multimodal pain management.
