Genetic Predisposition Meets Environmental Factors

joint 2026-07-16

Research confirms that many dog breeds have specific genetic predispositions to orthopaedic conditions like hip dysplasia, elbow dysplasia, and cruciate ligament rupture. A 2022 study by Binversie et al. investigated these genetic links using whole-genome sequencing. However, genetics are not destiny. A dog's nutrition, environment, exercise, and fitness levels play a significant role in their actual injury risk. This means that proactive measures, such as posture-based training and targeted rehabilitation, can powerfully mitigate genetic risks and improve long-term outcomes.
