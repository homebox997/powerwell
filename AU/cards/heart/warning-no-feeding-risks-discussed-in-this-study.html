Warning: No Feeding Risks Discussed in This Study

heart 2026-07-21

This research article does not address any feeding risks or dietary warnings for dogs with heart disease. It is a histopathologic study comparing left atrial tissue changes in dogs with dilated cardiomyopathy (DCM) and chronic mitral valve disease (CMVD), and does not investigate nutrition or feeding practices. However, it is well-known that certain diets (e.g., grain-free diets high in legumes) have been linked to DCM in some dogs. Pet owners should discuss appropriate diets with their veterinarian, especially for breeds predisposed to heart conditions, but this specific study provides no feeding-related guidance.

Source: A comparison of the histopathologic pattern of the left atrium in canine dilated cardiomyopathy and chronic mitral valve disease
