Complete guide: what I feed my 13-year-old staffy every single day

👤 Steve D. 📅 2026-07-11 🐕 Roscoe, Staffordshire Bull Terrier 💬 8 comments ❤️ 180 likes 👁 3650 views
Steve shares his complete daily feeding schedule for 13-year-old Staffy. Senior kibble, sardines, chicken, glucosamine. Dog is thriving with excellent blood results.
Roscoe's 13 and still going strong. I get a lot of questions about what he eats so here's my daily feeding breakdown. Morning: 1 cup senior kibble (low calorie because he's less active) + half a tin of wet food mixed in Afternoon: Sardines in spring water (small tin) - great for omega 3s Evening: 1 cup senior kibble + a spoonful of boiled chicken Plus: Glucosamine supplement in his dinner, fresh water changed 3 times a day He's maintained a healthy weight, coat's in great condition, vet says his bloods are excellent. I know every dog is different but happy to share more details if useful.
