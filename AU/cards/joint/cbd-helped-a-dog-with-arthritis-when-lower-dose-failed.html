CBD Helped a Dog with Arthritis When Lower Dose Failed

joint 2026-07-16

In a clinical trial included in the 2022 review, dogs with osteoarthritis were given CBD supplements. The study found that CBD showed positive outcomes in 6 out of 7 trials. However, one trial using a lower dose showed no effect, highlighting that dosage and formulation are critical. For example, a dog owner might see no improvement with a low-dose CBD product, but switching to a properly dosed, high-quality product from a company that provides a Certificate of Analysis could lead to significant pain relief. This case underscores the importance of veterinary guidance for dosing.
