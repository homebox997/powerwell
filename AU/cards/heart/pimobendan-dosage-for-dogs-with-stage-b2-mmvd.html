Pimobendan Dosage for Dogs with Stage B2 MMVD

heart 2026-07-18

In the study, dogs with Stage B2 myxomatous mitral valve disease received pimobendan at a standard dosage of 0.2–0.3 mg/kg orally twice daily. This dosing regimen is commonly used in veterinary cardiology to achieve optimal inotropic and vasodilatory effects. It is important to administer pimobendan consistently with or without food, as directed by a veterinarian. Dosage adjustments may be needed based on individual response and renal function, always under professional supervision.
