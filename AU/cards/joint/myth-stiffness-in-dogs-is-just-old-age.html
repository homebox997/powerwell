Myth: Stiffness in Dogs Is Just 'Old Age'

joint 2026-07-16

Many pet owners dismiss stiffness, limping, or reduced activity in older dogs as normal aging. In reality, these are often signs of osteoarthritis, a painful and treatable condition. Attributing these symptoms to old age delays diagnosis and treatment, allowing pain to worsen. Veterinary professionals recommend screening for OA in any senior dog showing mobility changes, as early management can significantly improve comfort and mobility.
