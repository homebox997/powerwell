Early Detection Through Client Questionnaires

joint 2026-07-16

Too often, dogs are diagnosed with osteoarthritis only after severe mobility loss. Simple tools like client questionnaires and musculoskeletal screening exams can help veterinary clinics identify signs of OA earlier. By planting awareness in the community, pet owners can detect joint pain before it becomes debilitating, allowing for earlier intervention and better long-term outcomes.
