Vet Insight: Holistic Approach to Joint Care

joint 2026-07-16

Veterinarians emphasize that managing canine osteoarthritis requires a multimodal approach. Dr. Sarah Miller, a veterinary orthopedic specialist, states: 'Combining weight control, appropriate exercise, joint supplements, and pain medication yields the best outcomes. Each dog's plan should be tailored to their specific needs and disease stage.' Regular re-evaluations are crucial to adjust treatments as the condition progresses.
