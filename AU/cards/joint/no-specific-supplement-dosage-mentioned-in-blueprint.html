No Specific Supplement Dosage Mentioned in Blueprint

joint 2026-07-16

The CARE OA Blueprint emphasizes a comprehensive management approach including weight optimization, exercise, and home adjustments, but does not provide specific supplement dosage guidelines. For supplement advice, pet owners should consult their veterinarian, who can recommend evidence-based options like omega-3 fatty acids, glucosamine, or chondroitin based on the dog's weight, OA stage, and overall health. Always follow veterinary guidance for safe dosing.
