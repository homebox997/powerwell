Licked his paws until they bled - finally got a proper diagnosis

👤 Sophie G. 📅 2026-07-11 🐕 Milo, Labradoodle 💬 8 comments ❤️ 47 likes 👁 680 views
Milo licked his paws for 2 years. Three vets, no answers. Saw a dermatologist, got proper allergy testing. Found grass seed and mould allergies.
Posting this because I WISH someone had told me this 2 years ago. Milo was licking his paws constantly. I thought it was anxiety, then allergies, then boredom. Took him to three vets. Tried elimination diets, Apoquel, everything. Finally went to a dermatologist. Turned out he had a grass seed allergy AND a environmental mould allergy. Two years of suffering because no one tested properly. If your dog is obsessively licking paws and you've tried everything - please consider a proper allergy test. It cost us $350 but we got answers. Milo is a different dog now.

Paw licking is THE classic sign of allergies in dogs. Environmental (atopy) is the most common cause — dust mites, grasses, pollens. Apoquel or Cytopoint are the most effective vet treatments.
