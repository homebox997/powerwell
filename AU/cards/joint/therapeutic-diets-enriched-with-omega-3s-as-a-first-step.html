Therapeutic Diets Enriched with Omega-3s as a First Step

joint 2026-07-16

Switching to a veterinarian-recommended therapeutic diet rich in omega-3 fatty acids is a powerful first step in preventing and managing canine and feline osteoarthritis. According to a 2022 meta-analysis, only 10% of trials showed no benefit from these diets, while the majority demonstrated clear improvements in mobility and comfort. These specially formulated foods provide a consistent, high-quality source of anti-inflammatory nutrients. For pets at risk of joint issues, starting this dietary change early can help delay the onset of arthritis symptoms.
