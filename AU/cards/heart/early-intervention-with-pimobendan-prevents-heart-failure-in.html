Early Intervention with Pimobendan Prevents Heart Failure in Dogs

heart 2026-07-18

Starting pimobendan therapy early in dogs with Stage B2 myxomatous mitral valve disease can prevent or delay the onset of congestive heart failure. The study showed that monotherapy with pimobendan reduced cardiac remodeling and improved heart function in asymptomatic dogs. Regular echocardiographic monitoring is recommended for at-risk breeds like Cavalier King Charles Spaniels to detect MMVD early and begin preventive treatment, potentially extending lifespan and quality of life.

Source: Echocardiographic Changes in Dogs with Stage B2 Myxomatous Mitral Valve Disease Treated with Pimobendan Monotherapy
