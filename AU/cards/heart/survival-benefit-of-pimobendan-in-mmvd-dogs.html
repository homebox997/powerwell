Survival Benefit of Pimobendan in MMVD Dogs

heart 2026-07-20

Clinical studies have shown that pimobendan significantly extends survival time in dogs with myxomatous mitral valve disease (MMVD) and congestive heart failure. In a landmark placebo-controlled trial, dogs receiving pimobendan had a median survival time of 267 days, compared to 140 days for those on placebo. This represents a nearly twofold increase in lifespan. Pimobendan also improved clinical signs such as coughing and exercise tolerance. These data strongly support its use as a first-line therapy in MMVD-related heart failure, as recommended by the ACVIM consensus guidelines.
