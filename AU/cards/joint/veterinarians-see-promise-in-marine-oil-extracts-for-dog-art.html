Veterinarians See Promise in Marine Oil Extracts for Dog Arthritis

joint 2026-07-16

Veterinary experts involved in the study noted that EAB-277 offers a well-tolerated alternative to NSAIDs for managing osteoarthritis pain. They emphasize that while NSAIDs are effective, they can cause gastrointestinal, renal, or hepatic side effects with long-term use. The study's findings suggest that natural extracts like green-lipped mussel and krill oil can provide similar pain relief with a better safety profile. Experts recommend these supplements as part of a multimodal approach to arthritis management, especially for dogs that cannot tolerate conventional medications.

Tags: veterinarian opinion, natural arthritis treatment, EAB-277 safety, NSAIDs alternatives, multimodal pain management
Source: Frontiers | Evaluation of the comparative efficacy of green lipped mussel plus krill oil extracts (EAB-277), Biota orientalis extracts or NSAIDs for the treatment of dogs with osteoarthritis associated pain: a blinded, placebo-controlled study
