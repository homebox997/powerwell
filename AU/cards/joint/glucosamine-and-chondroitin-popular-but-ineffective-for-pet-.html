Glucosamine and Chondroitin: Popular but Ineffective for Pet Arthritis

joint 2026-07-16

Despite being the most widely recommended joint supplements for pets, glucosamine and chondroitin have been consistently shown to be ineffective for managing osteoarthritis pain. A 2022 systematic review found that negative control groups (pets receiving no supplement) often did just as well or better. Furthermore, there is no research confirming that these supplements prevent arthritis or slow its progression. Pet owners should be aware that the popularity of these products is not backed by science, and they should no longer be recommended for pets with osteoarthritis.
