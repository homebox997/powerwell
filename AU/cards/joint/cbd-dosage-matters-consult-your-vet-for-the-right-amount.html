CBD Dosage Matters: Consult Your Vet for the Right Amount

joint 2026-07-16

The effectiveness of CBD for managing arthritis pain in dogs is highly dependent on dosage and formulation. A 2022 review found that while most CBD trials showed positive outcomes, one study using a lower dose showed no effect at all. This means giving a 'one-size-fits-all' amount may not work. Pet owners should consult their veterinarian to determine the appropriate dosage based on their dog's weight, severity of arthritis, and overall health. Additionally, always choose a product that provides a Certificate of Analysis (COA) to verify purity and potency.
