Worried I'm imagining it - or is my kelpie slowing down?

👤 Tom B. 📅 2026-07-11 🐕 Duke, Australian Kelpie 💬 8 comments ❤️ 211 likes 👁 2950 views
Duke the 7-year-old Kelpie seems stiffer in the mornings. Is 7 too young for arthritis signs? Seeking perspective from other kelpie owners.
Might be being overprotective here but bear with me. Duke's 7 and a working kelpie. He's still sharp as anything mentally but I've noticed over the past few months he's a bit stiffer when he first gets up from lying down. Doesn't limp or anything but there's definitely a moment where he takes a beat before he starts moving. Is 7 too young for arthritis? Or am I just overthinking it? My mate who also has kelpies said 7 is still young but I'm not sure. Not looking to rush to the vet for nothing but also don't want to ignore something if it's the start of something.

8 Comments

Sue P. 2026-07-11

Kelpies hide joint pain SO well because they're working dogs. 7 is actually not unusual for early stiffness in active breeds. Get the x-rays done — don't wait like we did with our girl.

Rob F. 2026-07-11

Our border collie started slowing at 7 too. Turns out she had early hip dysplasia we had no idea about. Ultrasound + blood panel first, then x-rays if needed.

Gary M. 2026-07-11

GLA:D program for dogs is a thing! It's a structured rehab program specifically for OA. Some physio clinics offer it in Aus. Might be worth looking into for a 7yo working dog.

Karen H. 2026-07-11

Kelpies are incredible athletes but their hips/knees take a beating from all that running. We started our girl on Osteo Advantage from the vet at 7 and it's been great.

Dave T. 2026-07-11

Check if your kelpie is limping after rest — that's a classic OA sign. Ours would be stiff for the first 10 mins in the morning then loosen up. Progressive OA unfortunately.

Tracy L. 2026-07-11

Our golden was 6 when we first noticed stiffness. Regular short walks rather than big weekend sessions made a huge difference. Pacing is underrated.

Nina B. 2026-07-11

Kelpies are so tough they often don't show pain until it's advanced. Good on you for getting it checked. Early intervention is everything.

Brett C. 2026-07-11

Has anyone tried Dasuquin supplements? We switched from Glucosamine/Chondroitin to Dasuquin and noticed better results within 3 weeks.
