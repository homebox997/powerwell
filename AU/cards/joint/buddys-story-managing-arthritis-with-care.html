Buddy's Story: Managing Arthritis with Care

joint 2026-07-16

Buddy, a 10-year-old Labrador Retriever, began showing stiffness after walks and struggled to climb stairs. His owner noticed him whimpering when getting up. After a veterinary diagnosis of osteoarthritis, Buddy started a regimen of prescribed NSAIDs, joint supplements, and a weight management plan. Within three months, Buddy was more active and willing to play. His owner emphasizes that consistent care and vet follow-ups made a significant difference.
