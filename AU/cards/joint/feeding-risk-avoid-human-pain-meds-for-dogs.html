Feeding Risk: Avoid Human Pain Meds for Dogs

joint 2026-07-16

Never give human pain relievers like ibuprofen, acetaminophen, or aspirin to dogs. These drugs can cause severe side effects, including gastrointestinal ulcers, liver damage, and kidney failure. For example, ibuprofen is highly toxic to dogs even in small doses. Only use veterinarian-prescribed medications specifically formulated for canine osteoarthritis, and always follow dosage instructions carefully.
