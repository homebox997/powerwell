Always Talk to Your Vet Before Starting Joint Supplements

joint 2026-07-16

According to CARE experts, the most important step before starting any joint supplement or diet change for your pet is to consult your veterinarian. What works best will depend on your pet's specific health profile, including their age, weight, severity of arthritis, and any underlying conditions like kidney or liver disease. Your vet can recommend science-backed products, appropriate dosages, and help you avoid ineffective or potentially harmful supplements. Pet owners should not rely on marketing claims alone—professional guidance is essential for safe and effective arthritis management.
