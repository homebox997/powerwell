Cardiologist visit cost breakdown - so you know what to expect

👤 Nicole F. 📅 2026-07-11 🐕 Freddie, Miniature Schnauzer 💬 8 comments ❤️ 61 likes 👁 1180 views
Honest cost breakdown from a recent cardiologist visit for Freddie's heart condition. $480 initial consult, $95 tests, $120 follow-up.
Just got back from a specialist cardiology appointment with Freddie and wanted to share the cost breakdown because I went in completely blind and was shocked by the bill. Initial consult with echocardiogram: $480 Blood tests (proBNP): $95 Follow-up consult: $120 Monthly vet check: $65 Total first year is going to be around $1,400 with medications on top. I love Freddie more than anything but if you're budgeting for this - factor in at least $500 for the first visit alone. Happy to answer questions about what the process was like if anyone is heading that direction.
