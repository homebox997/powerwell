Glucosamine Dosage Guide for Dogs: Safe and Effective Use

heart 2026-07-18

Glucosamine is a common joint supplement for dogs, but correct dosing matters. The typical recommended dose is 20-25 mg per pound of body weight per day, divided into two doses. For a 50-pound dog, that means about 500-625 mg twice daily. Chondroitin is often combined at 15-20 mg per pound daily. Use veterinary-formulated products, as human supplements may contain xylitol or incorrect concentrations. It may take 4-8 weeks to see improvement. Always consult your vet before starting supplements, especially for dogs on other medications.
