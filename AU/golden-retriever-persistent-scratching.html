Golden retriever won't stop scratching - we've tried everything

👤 Michelle T. 📅 2026-07-11 🐕 Archie, Golden Retriever 💬 8 comments ❤️ 178 likes 👁 1790 views
7-year-old Golden Retriever scratching for 4 months. Vet found no parasites. Steroids helped temporarily. Food change didn't work.
Archie has been scratching himself raw for about 4 months now. We're at our wits end. He's broken the skin on his back legs and sides. The vet gave us steroids which helped short-term but it came back as soon as we finished the course. She's done skin scrapings and said no mites or parasites. We've changed his food to salmon and rice (expensive stuff), we've tried oat baths, coconut oil, everything at the pet shop. Nothing works long-term. Has anyone dealt with persistent scratching in Golden Retrievers? Is there a specialist we should see? I feel terrible for him.
