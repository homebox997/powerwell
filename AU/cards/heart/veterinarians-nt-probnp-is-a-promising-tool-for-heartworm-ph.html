Veterinarians: NT-proBNP Is a Promising Tool for Heartworm PH Assessment

heart 2026-07-21

According to researchers at the University of Las Palmas de Gran Canaria, measuring serum NT-proBNP concentration can be a valuable addition to the diagnostic workup for dogs with heartworm disease. This biomarker helps identify animals with moderate to severe precapillary pulmonary hypertension (PH), especially when echocardiography is inconclusive or unavailable. The study authors note that NT-proBNP levels reflect right ventricular stretch and can aid in staging disease severity. They recommend its use alongside echocardiography to improve detection and monitoring of PH in heartworm-infected dogs.
